Conlang Club propaganda

Our flags, posters, and other propaganda are available from this page.

Flag

The Seattle Conlang Club flag is a fusion of the Conlang Flag, which depicts the Tower of Babel, and the ring of waves representing the Puget Sound that's featured on the unofficial Contemporary Seattle Flag.

The overall design was created by Frederick Chan and was made the official SCC flag on June 16, 2024.
